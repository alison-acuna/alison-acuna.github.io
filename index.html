About

I love learning. As a student coach at InsideTrack, I have the opportunity to support students at Arizona State University and Brandman University in navigating their Master's degree. After 5 years of helping others take the next steps in their education, I was anxious to take the next step in mine.

I am drawn to web development because there are always new things to learn. I am also drawn to the opportunity to create and improve on that creation. Web development is an industry filled with interesting challenges. Since I take a lot of satisfaction from doing a hard thing well, web development is a really appealing field in which to focus my learning.

Prior to entering a development bootcamp, I spent a year learning HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Javascript, Python and Django independently using Treehouse and CodeAcademy. I am good at teaching myself to code. I decided to enter a bootcamp to learn faster and get feedback about my code.
